Why use our DApp

Step 1:Make MetaMask Account

Your document's hash is checked against existing records. If it matches, you will see a verification success message, ensuring your document's authenticity.

Step 2: Upload Your File

Select a file from your device, enter a unique ID, and click "Upload to IPFS and Store on Blockchain". This process will upload your file to IPFS and store the reference on the blockchain.

Step 3: Verification

Your document's hash is checked against existing records. If it matches, you will see a verification success message, ensuring your document's authenticity.

Step 4: Retrieve Your File

To retrieve a file, enter the same ID number used during upload. The IPFS hash stored on the blockchain will be used to fetch your file directly from IPFS.
